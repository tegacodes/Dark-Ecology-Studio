This exhibition features works created in response to field research of the wastewater, energy, communications and waste disposal systems of Purchase College and Westchester. The resultant field guides and sound works are comprised of recordings and research done on the Purchase campus and during site visits to Westchester recycling plant, Waste water treatment plant and the Indian Point Nuclear Power Plant.
